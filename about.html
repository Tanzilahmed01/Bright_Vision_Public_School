About Our School

Established with a vision to provide quality education to every child — Rishika Nagar, Khargone

Our Story

Bright Vision Public School was established with a simple but powerful mission — to give every child in our community access to quality education in a nurturing and inspiring environment. Starting with just two classrooms, we have grown into a full-fledged institution serving students from Nursery to Grade 8.

Our school follows a holistic approach combining academic excellence with character development, creative expression, and physical well-being. We believe every child is unique and deserves personalized attention.
